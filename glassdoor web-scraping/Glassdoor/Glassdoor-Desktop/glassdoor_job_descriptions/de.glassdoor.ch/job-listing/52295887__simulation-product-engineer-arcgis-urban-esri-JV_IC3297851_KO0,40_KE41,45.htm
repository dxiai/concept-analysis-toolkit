    Simulation Product Engineer - ArcGIS Urban

    3.6 Esri – Zürich vor 10 Tagen
    Bewerbung erfolgt 10.11.19
    Jetzt bewerben
    vor 10 Tagen
    • Job

    Overview

    Are you passionate about creating good user experiences on the web? So are we! In the past few years, Esri has put a large effort into making design tools accessible and powerful. We are dedicated to continually pushing the boundaries of urban design and planning, and you can play a key role in our mission. At the Esri R&D Center-Zurich, we develop innovative urban planning software such as CityEngine and the new ArcGIS Urban. We are looking for creative, smart engineers to help us build the next generation of urban planning tools. As a software engineer at Esri you will be solving challenges that make a difference in the world.
    Responsibilities:
    • Lead the vision and design of new simulation tools for ArcGIS Urban
    • Design, create, and maintain key supporting 2D/3D GIS datasets and services
    • Create compelling product samples, blogs, articles, documentation, and tutorials to showcase new features and novel usage patterns of ArcGIS Urban
    • Establish and define customer requirements; translate them into potential product improvements and new software designs
    • Prepare demos and presentations for international conferences
    • Work in a small and dynamic team of people passionate about what they do
    • Help shape the future of urban planning

    Requirements

    • 2+ years of professional experience with geospatial analysis and simulation
    • Excellent written and verbal communication skills, including presentation skills
    • Passion for quality, detail oriented, and know how to break software by finding bugs
    • Bachelor’s in geomatics engineering, geography, computer science, or a related field
    • Existing work authorization for Switzerland
    Recommended Qualifications:
    • Experience with (or interest in) simulation topics such as traffic, wind, solar potential, shadow analysis, crowd simulation
    • Familiarity with ArcGIS or GIS in general
    • 2+ years of industry experience in software QA, ideally of JavaScript web application
    • Experience in test automation and developing test scripts
    • Expertise in online mapping and/or data visualization
    Our Zurich team enjoys:
    • Free lunches and fresh fruit
    • Free coffee, tea, and soft drinks
    • Fun team activities
    • Competitive salary
    • 40-hour work week
    • Flexible working hours
    • Dynamic, fast-growing international team

    The Company



    Our passion for improving quality of life through geography is at the heart of everything we do. Esri’s geographic information system (GIS) technology inspires and enables governments, universities, and businesses worldwide to save money, lives, and our environment through a deeper understanding of the changing world around them.

    Carefully managed growth and zero debt give Esri stability that is uncommon in today's volatile business world. Privately held, we offer exceptional benefits, competitive salaries, 401(k) and profit-sharing programs, opportunities for personal and professional growth, and much more.

    If you need a reasonable accommodation for any part of the employment process, please email humanresources@esri.com and let us know the nature of your request and your contact information.
